Our Story

Our journey started with a simple yet powerful belief:

"Every Child deserves an Opportunity to Ignite their Inner Strength."

In 2013, we started training children with special needs and various disabilities, using swimming as a therapeutic tool. Gradually, we were able to witness significant improvements in their overall development which drew parental interest and a surge in enrollment. This surge raised awareness about its effectiveness and benefits of swimming, particularly enhancing motor coordination, managing hyperactivity, focus, sleep, speech, social and emotional regulation.

About Us

AKSA FOUNDATION is a non-profit organization committed to creating a world-class integrated center for children with special needs and diverse disabilities.

Our objective is to empower young minds to unlock their fullest potential and live independently with dignity, honor, and self-respect.
