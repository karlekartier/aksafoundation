Celebrating Unity and Gratitude! 🌾🙌

Pongal wasn’t just a festival at AKSA Foundation; it was a celebration of unity, tradition, and the incredible spirit of our special students in Chennai. The harvest festival of Pongal was marked with great fervor and joy as we came together to celebrate our roots and the blessings of nature.

The celebration included the traditional preparation of "Sweet Pongal" in a ceramic pot, with students actively participating in the ritual. Every child felt part of the process, from helping with decorations to enjoying the festive meal together, fostering a sense of community and shared gratitude.

"Pongal at AKSA is a beautiful reminder of our collective gratitude for the harvest and the joy of coming together as one family."
Team AKSA Foundation

We believe in the importance of cultural celebrations for the holistic development of our students. Our Pongal event allowed children to experience the rich traditions of Tamil Nadu while feeling included and valued in every activity.

Celebration Highlights:

  • Traditional Pongal preparation with student participation.
  • Festive decorations including sugarcane and colorful Kolams.
  • Shared festive lunch for all students, staff, and guests.

Thank you to all who joined us in this harvest celebration. Let’s continue nurturing a spirit of unity and joy for our children, ensuring they feel the warmth of our traditions every day.
