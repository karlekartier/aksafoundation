About AKSA Foundation

AKSA FOUNDATION is a non-profit organization committed to creating a world-class integrated center for children with special needs and diverse disabilities.

Our objective is to empower young minds to unlock their fullest potential and live independently with dignity, honor, and self-respect. We provide a safe and valued environment where every child can flourish.

Learning Centre Vocational Centre Sports Academy

Our institutions provide an inclusive and supportive environment specifically designed to cater to the unique needs and aspirations of learners, ensuring their holistic development and empowerment.

Our Vision

To establish a world-class integrated center providing specialized facilities where every child is respected, valued, and empowered with the skills to lead a fulfilling life.

Our Mission

To provide innovative resources and a multifaceted approach that nurtures children with diverse needs, ensuring their full participation in society with excellence.

Our Objective

To empower young minds to unlock their potential and lead independent lives with dignity and self-respect through dedicated educational and therapeutic support.

Empowering young minds with disabilities through integrated learning and therapeutic sports in Chennai.
