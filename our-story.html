Our Legacy of Inclusion

From a single vision in 2013 to a comprehensive academy of hope and world-class athletic feats.

"Every child deserves an opportunity to ignite their inner strength."

— The Governing Belief of AKSA Foundation

Our Journey

Milestones of Progress

2013: The Genesis

Started training children with special needs using swimming as a primary therapeutic tool to enhance motor coordination and social regulation.

The Discovery

Witnessed significant improvements in hyperactivity, sleep, and speech, leading to a surge in parental trust and enrolment.

National & Global Impact

Our athletes began excelling in National/International competitions and achieving World Records in Sea Swimming Expeditions.

Comprehensive Vision

Broadened services by introducing a dedicated learning center to support academic, behavioral, and social challenges.

Official Identity

Formally registered as AKSA FOUNDATION, solidifying our mission to provide integrated services under one roof.

World Class Athletes

Specialized training programs producing National & International medalists and World Record holders in open-sea swimming.

Integrated Facility

A comprehensive campus in Chennai offering Learning, Vocational training, and Therapeutic Sports—all under one roof.

Empowering young minds with disabilities through integrated learning and therapeutic sports in Chennai.
