Emoji Day Celebration at AKSA

  1. Home
  2. Events
  3. Emoji Day
Celebration

Spreading Smiles one Emoji at a time! 😃🎉

AKSA Foundation turned into a world of smiles and giggles as we celebrated World Emoji Day with our little stars! The day was filled with joy, creativity, and expressive learning as students engaged in various activities designed to explore emotions and facial expressions.

Our dedicated teachers organized a special workshop where children created their own emoji masks and props. From the classic "Happy Face" to the "Cool Shades" emoji, every child found a way to represent their feelings through art.

"Events like Emoji Day are more than just fun; they are vital tools for emotional development and social interaction for our children."
Team AKSA Foundation

The celebration also included interactive games where children matched emojis to different social scenarios, helping them better understand and communicate their emotions—a key part of our holistic development program at the AKSA Learning Centre.

Highlights of the Day:

  • Interactive emotion-matching games for cognitive development.
  • DIY Emoji mask-making workshop.
  • Group photo sessions with giant emoji cutouts.

We are grateful to our staff and volunteers who made this day possible. Witnessing the joy and pure expression of our students reminds us why our mission at Shenoy Nagar is so important.
